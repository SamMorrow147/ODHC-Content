## Patient Portal

### Access Your Health Information Online

The Open Door Health Center Patient Portal gives you secure, convenient access to your health information anytime, anywhere.

### What You Can Do

With the patient portal, you can:
- View your medical records and test results
- Request prescription refills
- Schedule appointments
- Communicate with your care team
- Update your contact information
- View billing statements
- Review visit summaries

### How to Access

To access the patient portal, you'll need to register for an account. Contact our office at **(507) 388-2120** to get started.

### Secure and Confidential

Your health information is protected with industry-standard security measures. Your portal access is private and confidential.

### Need Help?

If you have questions about the patient portal or need assistance accessing your account, please call us at (507) 388-2120 and our staff will be happy to help you.
