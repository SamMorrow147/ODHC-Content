## Patient Forms

### New Patient and Registration Forms

To help make your visit more efficient, please download and complete any necessary forms before your appointment.

### Available Forms

**New Patient Forms**
- New patient registration form
- Medical history questionnaire
- Dental history form
- Behavioral health intake form

**Financial Assistance**
- Sliding Fee Discount Program Application
- Self-Attestation Form for Income Documentation

**Employment**
- Employment Application (fillable PDF available)
- Volunteer/Intern Application

### Bring to Your Appointment

When you come for your first visit, please bring:
- Photo ID
- Insurance card(s)
- List of current medications
- Any relevant medical records from previous providers

### Questions?

If you have questions about which forms you need to complete, please call us at **(507) 388-2120** and our staff will assist you.
