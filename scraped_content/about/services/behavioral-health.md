## Behavioral Health

### Helping individuals and families with a wide variety of mental health needs

Open Door Health Center provides comprehensive behavioral health services to support the mental health and well-being of individuals and families in our community. Our licensed therapists and counselors offer compassionate, confidential care in a welcoming environment.

### Services Offered

Our behavioral health team provides:
- Individual therapy/counseling
- Family therapy
- Couples counseling
- Child and adolescent counseling
- Mental health assessments
- Depression and anxiety treatment
- Stress management
- Grief counseling
- Trauma-informed care
- Medication management (in coordination with medical providers)

### Integrated Care Approach

We believe in treating the whole person. Our behavioral health services are integrated with our medical and dental care, ensuring comprehensive treatment that addresses all aspects of your health.

### Confidential Care

All behavioral health services are completely confidential and provided in accordance with HIPAA privacy regulations.

### Insurance and Payment

We accept most private insurance plans, Minnesota Care, and Medical Assistance (MA). Our sliding fee discount program is available for those who qualify, ensuring that mental health care is accessible to everyone who needs it.
