## Dental Health

### We offer affordable dental care in several clinics in southern Minnesota

Open Door Health Center provides comprehensive dental services to help maintain and improve your oral health. Our experienced dental team is committed to providing quality, affordable dental care to the community.

### Services Offered

Our dental health team provides:
- Preventive dental care
- Routine cleanings and exams
- Fillings and restorations
- Extractions
- Emergency dental care
- Pediatric dentistry
- Oral health education

### Dental Emergency Care

**Dental Emergencies**: Call **(507) 388-2120** to be screened for an emergency dental appointment.

### Insurance and Payment

We accept many private insurance plans including Delta Dental. Minnesota Care and Medical Assistance (MA) are also accepted. For those without insurance or with high deductibles, our sliding fee discount program may help make dental care affordable.
