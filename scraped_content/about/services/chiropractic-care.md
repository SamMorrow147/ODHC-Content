## Chiropractic Care

### Comprehensive chiropractic services for pain relief and wellness

Open Door Health Center offers chiropractic care as part of our commitment to providing comprehensive healthcare services to our community.

### What is Chiropractic Care?

Chiropractic care focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of neuromuscular disorders, with an emphasis on treatment through manual adjustment and/or manipulation of the spine. Chiropractors aim to reduce pain and improve the functionality of patients as well as to educate them on how they can account for their own health via exercise, ergonomics, and other therapies.

### Conditions Treated

Chiropractic care can help with:
- Back pain
- Neck pain
- Headaches and migraines
- Joint pain and stiffness
- Sports injuries
- Work-related injuries
- Muscle strain
- Posture problems
