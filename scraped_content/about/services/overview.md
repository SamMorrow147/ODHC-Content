## Our Services

Open Door Health Center provides comprehensive healthcare services under one roof. Our integrated approach ensures that all aspects of your health are addressed with quality, compassionate care.

### Medical Health

Our caring staff are focused on all of your health care needs. We provide primary care services including preventive care, chronic disease management, acute illness treatment, and more.

[Learn more about Medical Health →](medical.md)

### Dental Health

We offer affordable dental care in several clinics in southern Minnesota. From preventive care to emergency services, our dental team is here to help maintain your oral health.

[Learn more about Dental Health →](dental.md)

### Behavioral Health

Helping individuals and families with a wide variety of mental health needs. Our licensed therapists provide confidential counseling and therapy services in a supportive environment.

[Learn more about Behavioral Health →](behavioral-health.md)

### Insurance Enrollment

We provide free assistance with health insurance enrollment, helping you navigate options and enroll in the plan that's right for you.

[Learn more about Insurance Enrollment →](insurance-enrollment.md)

### Chiropractic Care

Comprehensive chiropractic services for pain relief and wellness, addressing back pain, neck pain, and other musculoskeletal issues.

[Learn more about Chiropractic Care →](chiropractic-care.md)

## Why Choose Open Door Health Center?

- **Comprehensive Care**: Medical, dental, and behavioral health services all in one location
- **Affordable**: Sliding fee discount program and financial assistance available
- **Accessible**: We accept most insurance plans including Minnesota Care and Medical Assistance
- **Quality**: Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) with experienced providers
- **Community-Focused**: Serving all people in Southern Minnesota regardless of ability to pay
