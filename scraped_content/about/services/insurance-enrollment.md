## Insurance Enrollment Assistance

### We can help you navigate health insurance options

Open Door Health Center provides free assistance with health insurance enrollment. Our knowledgeable staff can help you understand your options and enroll in the health insurance plan that's right for you and your family.

### How We Can Help

- Understanding your health insurance options
- Enrollment assistance for Minnesota Health Insurance Marketplace
- MNsure enrollment support
- Medical Assistance (MA) applications
- MinnesotaCare applications
- Answering questions about coverage and benefits
- Annual renewal assistance

### Who Can Get Help?

Everyone is welcome to receive enrollment assistance, whether or not you're a current patient at Open Door Health Center.

### Insurance Plans We Accept

Most private insurance plans are accepted, including:
- Aetna
- Blue Cross Blue Shield of MN
- Health Partners
- Medica
- Medicare
- Medicaid
- Preferred One
- Prime West Health
- United Health Care
- And many others

We also accept:
- Minnesota Care
- Medical Assistance (MA)
