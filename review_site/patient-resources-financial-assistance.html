Affordable Care for Everyone

Don't let cost keep you from getting the care you need, we offer sliding fee discounts, payment plans, and help with insurance enrollment.

We provide care regardless of insurance status. Sliding fee discounts are available for those who qualify. We're committed to making quality healthcare accessible and affordable for all families in our community.

Sliding Fee Discount Program

We offer five levels of discounts based on household income and size. Discount levels are determined during an in-person interview with our Patient Services team. We do not quote discount levels over the phone.
