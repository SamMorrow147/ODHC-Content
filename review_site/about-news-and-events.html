News and Events

Stay Connected with Open Door Health Center

Keep up with the latest news, announcements, and community events from Open Door Health Center. We're always working to expand our services, improve access to care, and support the health of our community.

Recent News

Shakopee Location Now Open

We're excited to announce the opening of our new Shakopee location, bringing full-service medical, dental, and behavioral health care to Shakopee-area families.

Expanded Hours Available

To better serve our patients, we've expanded our appointment availability. Contact us to schedule at a time that works for you.

New Providers Joining Our Team

We're pleased to welcome new medical and dental providers to our team, helping us serve more patients across our locations.

Community Events

Health Fairs and Screenings

Open Door Health Center participates in community health fairs and offers free health screenings throughout the year. Check back for upcoming dates and locations.

Educational Workshops

We offer educational sessions on topics like diabetes management, nutrition, mental health, and preventive care. Contact us for information on upcoming workshops.

Stay Informed

Want to receive updates about Open Door Health Center news and events? Contact our main office at (507) 388-2120 to be added to our mailing list.
