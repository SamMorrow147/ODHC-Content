Quality Healthcare for Everyone in Southern Minnesota

Affordable medical, dental, and behavioral health care for people of all ages.

Open Door Health Center is an independently owned, nonprofit community health center. We accept most private insurance, Minnesota Care, and Medical Assistance. Sliding fee discounts are available for those who qualify.

Call to schedule: (507) 388-2120

1. Hero Section

Value Proposition

Quality, compassionate care for your whole family. We treat the whole person with coordinated medical, dental, and behavioral health services under one roof.

3. Services

Medical Health

Primary care for adults and children, preventive care, chronic disease management, immunizations, and women's health services.

Dental Health

Preventive care, cleanings, fillings, extractions, pediatric dentistry, and emergency dental care.

Behavioral Health

Individual and family counseling, assessments, depression and anxiety treatment, stress management, and trauma-informed care.

4. Trust Indicators

  • Insurance Accepted: Most private plans, Minnesota Care, and Medical Assistance
  • Sliding Fee Program: Discounts based on income and household size
  • No One Turned Away: Care regardless of ability to pay
  • Language Services: Free assistance available

5. Additional Services

Insurance Enrollment Help: Guidance to find coverage that fits your needs.

Chiropractic Care: Natural pain relief and whole-body wellness support.

6. Why Choose Open Door?

  • Community-Focused: Independent nonprofit serving Southern Minnesota families
  • Comprehensive Care: Medical, dental, and behavioral health in one place
  • Affordable: Sliding fee discounts and broad insurance acceptance
  • Integrated Care: Coordinated treatment across services

7. Patient Resources

Quick access to what you need:

  • Patient Portal: Access your records and communicate with your care team
  • Forms: Download and complete forms before your visit
  • Financial Assistance: Learn about sliding fee discounts and payment help
  • Community Resources: Local support services and partnerships

8. Footer (Required Disclosure)

Open Door Health Center is a Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC). Open Door Health Center receives HHS funding and is a Health Center Program grantee under 42 U.S.C 254b, and is deemed a Public Health Service employer under 42 U.S.C. 233 (g)-(n).
