Medical Health

Our caring staff are focused on all of your health care needs

Open Door Health Center provides comprehensive medical health services to patients of all ages in Southern Minnesota. Our qualified, compassionate providers are dedicated to delivering quality healthcare to everyone, regardless of their ability to pay.

Services Offered

Our medical health team provides: - Primary care for adults and children - Preventive care and wellness exams - Chronic disease management - Acute illness treatment - Health screenings - Immunizations - Women's health services - Pediatric care

Insurance and Payment

Many private insurance plans are accepted, as well as Minnesota Care and Medical Assistance (MA). Individuals with high deductible insurance and those without insurance may be eligible for discounts based on our sliding fee program.
