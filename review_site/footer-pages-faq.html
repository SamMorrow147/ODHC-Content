    Appointments & Scheduling

    How do I schedule an appointment?

    Call (507) 388-2120 or use our online patient portal to schedule appointments with medical, dental, or behavioral health providers.

    Do you accept walk-ins?

    We accept walk-ins for dental emergencies. For other services, appointments are recommended but we'll do our best to accommodate urgent needs.

    Insurance & Payment

    What insurance do you accept?

    We accept most major insurance including Medical Assistance, Medicare, Minnesota Care, and most private insurance plans.

    What if I don't have insurance?

    We offer sliding fee discounts based on household income and family size. No one is turned away due to inability to pay.

    Services & Care

    What services do you offer?

    Medical, dental, behavioral health, and chiropractic care for all ages

    Do you see children?

    Yes, we provide care for patients of all ages, from infants to seniors

    Do I need to be a patient?

    New patients are always welcome‚Äîno referral needed to establish care

    Locations & Hours

    Where are you located?

    We have locations in Mankato and Shakopee, plus school-based health centers in local schools.

    What are your hours?

    Mon-Wed & Fri: 8am-5pm, Thu: 9am-5pm. Hours may vary by location‚Äîcall ahead to confirm.

    Patient Portal & Records

    How do I access my medical records?

    Sign up for our secure patient portal to view test results, request prescription refills, and message your care team.

    Can I get a copy of my records?

    Yes, contact our health information management team to request copies of your medical records.
