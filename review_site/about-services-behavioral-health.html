    Behavioral Health

    Helping individuals and families with a wide variety of mental health needs

    Open Door Health Center provides comprehensive behavioral health services to support the mental health and well-being of individuals and families in our community. Our licensed therapists and counselors offer compassionate, confidential care in a welcoming environment.

    Services Offered

    Our behavioral health team provides: - Individual therapy/counseling - Family therapy - Couples counseling - Child and adolescent counseling - Mental health assessments - Depression and anxiety treatment - Stress management - Grief counseling - Trauma-informed care - Medication management (in coordination with medical providers)

    Integrated Care Approach

    We believe in treating the whole person. Our behavioral health services are integrated with our medical and dental care, ensuring comprehensive treatment that addresses all aspects of your health.

    Schedule an Appointment

    Call us to schedule a behavioral health appointment: (507) 388-2120

    Available at our Mankato location: - Address: 309 Holly Lane, Mankato, MN 56001 - Hours: Monday-Wednesday & Friday: 8am-5pm, Thursday: 9am-5pm

    Confidential Care

    All behavioral health services are completely confidential and provided in accordance with HIPAA privacy regulations.

    Insurance and Payment

    We accept most private insurance plans, Minnesota Care, and Medical Assistance (MA). Our sliding fee discount program is available for those who qualify, ensuring that mental health care is accessible to everyone who needs it.

    You Don't Have to Face It Alone

    Compassionate mental health support for individuals and families in a safe, welcoming environment.

    Schedule Counseling Appointment
    Call (507) 388-2120

    Support When You Need It Most

    Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship challenges, or life transitions, our licensed therapists and counselors are here to help. You deserve to feel better, and we're here to support your journey.

    Behavioral Health Services

    • Individual Therapy - One-on-one counseling tailored to your needs
    • Family Therapy - Strengthen relationships and improve communication
    • Couples Counseling - Build a healthier partnership together
    • Child & Adolescent Counseling - Age-appropriate support for young people
    • Depression & Anxiety Treatment - Evidence-based approaches to feeling better
    • Stress Management - Learn coping strategies for daily challenges
    • Grief Counseling - Support through loss and difficult transitions
    • Trauma-Informed Care - Compassionate treatment for past trauma
    • Medication Management - Coordinated with our medical providers when needed

    A Whole-Person Approach

    Your mental health affects your overall wellbeing. That's why our behavioral health services work hand-in-hand with our medical and dental teams. When all your providers collaborate, you get better, more complete care.

    Your Privacy is Protected: All behavioral health services are completely confidential and provided in accordance with HIPAA privacy regulations. What you share stays private.
